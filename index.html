Download the app

Download the Rhino E-Bike app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

Locate a Rhino

Use the Rhino app to find the closest vehicle to you or look out for a Rhino Zone around you.

Scan the QR code

To unlock the vehicle, simply scan the QR code located on the panel.

Ride Safely

Enjoy the Rhino ride but remember to abide by all the traffic laws and parking policies.

End your ride

To end your ride, park the vehicle at a Yulu Zone, lock it and click on the End button on your app.
