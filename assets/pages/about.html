About Us

About Rhino E-Bike Rental Services At Rhino E-Bike Rental Services, our mission is to transform urban mobility in Guwahati by making transportation sustainable, accessible, and affordable. We began with the goal of reducing traffic congestion and pollution, and today, we are reshaping the way people move around the city. Guided by the principles of Accessibility, Availability, and Affordability, Rhino E-Bike is dedicated to empowering the people of Guwahati with eco-friendly, efficient commuting options. Our technology-driven platform integrates urban mobility, providing first and last-mile connectivity through our small electric vehicles (EVs). Using our intuitive mobile app, customers can seamlessly rent, ride, and return our GPS-enabled e-bikes, making travel across Guwahati more convenient and environmentally conscious. As a company rooted in Assam, we are passionate about contributing to the community. Every member of our team is committed to delivering a positive impact on urban living.
